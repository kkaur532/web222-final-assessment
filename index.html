Welcome to our Ludo site! Ludo is a classic board game that people of all ages can enjoy. The game is played with two to four people, and the goal is to be the first person to move all of your pieces from the beginning to the end. Each player takes turns rolling a die and repositioning their pieces. Ludo is a simple game that can be enjoyed in a social setting or as a family pastime. Ludo has been a popular online game to play with friends and family from all around the world since the advent of online gaming.
