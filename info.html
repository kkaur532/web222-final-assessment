Rules of the game

  4. Each player takes turns rolling a single die, with the highest number going first.
  5. Players must move their pieces in accordance with the number rolled on the die. If a player rolls a six, he or she may move a piece from their starting area or a piece already on the board six spaces.
  6. If a player's piece lands on a space where an opponent's piece already exists, the opponent's piece is returned to the starting area.
  7. The goal of a player is to move all four of his or her pieces from the beginning area to the home area.
  8. The game is won by the person who moves all of their pieces to their home region first.
